Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 25, 2026

Flickd ("we", "our", "us") operates the Flickd mobile application. This page explains what data we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account information: Your email address and password are handled by Supabase authentication when you create an account.
  • Preferences: Your selected region and genre preferences.
  • Usage data: Your swipe history and watchlist items.
  • Diagnostics: Limited technical logs needed to keep the service reliable.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To save your account, watchlist, and preferences across devices.
  • To personalize the recommendation deck based on the region and genres you choose.
  • To improve app quality, investigate bugs, and provide support.

3. What We Do Not Do

  • We do not sell your personal information.
  • We do not use third-party ad networks or cross-app tracking in this build.
  • We do not collect payment card information in the app.

4. Third-Party Services

  • Supabase (supabase.com): Authentication and database hosting. See Supabase Privacy Policy.

5. Data Retention

We retain your account data while your account is active. If you delete your account in the app, we permanently delete your associated profile, swipe history, and watchlist data from our backend.

6. Your Rights

  • Access: You can view your saved watchlist and preferences in the app.
  • Deletion: You can delete your account and associated data in Settings.
  • Support: Contact us if you need help with your data.

7. Children's Privacy

Flickd is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

8. Security

We use encrypted connections, account authentication, and backend access controls designed to protect your data. No system is perfect, but we work to keep your information secure.

9. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at support@flickd.app.
